Let's eat!

1. You will be given a random spending allowance at the start of the game.

2. There are four items below. By clicking on a food item you will add a specific amount of money to your spending.

3. You win the game by matching your total score to your spending allowance, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

4. The value of each food items is hidden from you, until you click on it.

5. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each food items.
